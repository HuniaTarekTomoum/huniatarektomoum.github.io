REVIVALISM
Azbakeya book Market

Competition
Cairo University
2008

Group work
:::Anais Mikaelian
:::Hunia Tomoum
:::Laila Selim

The idea was to regain the excitement, delight and appreciation to the place within the given compact program. The idea was achieved by interrupting the balance and creating varying rhythms (Chaos within Order), enhancing interaction and creating tension link between the volumes. This project is a renewal of the existing market "Azbakeya wall" for used books. It takes advantage of the fact that this place is inscribed in the historical center of Cairo. The adjacency of the cultural and social buildings in the past to the Azbakeya garden, was one of the factors for the phenomena of the book sellers on the Azbakeya wall. Our goal was to integrate our idea with the surrounding forces of the past, present and future. Views, entrances, and constraints were considered.


Nowadays sources of knowledge has become more diverse in this new age of information and technology, that a flat paper previously placed in a book is now being exploited to adapt to the uses of this new age. The project is then oriented to the possible manipulation of a paper, inspired by the Japanese art of paper folding called "Origami".
