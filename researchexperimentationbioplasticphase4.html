Digital Deposition

Team:
 Hunia Tomoum

Digital deposition was done by attaching a depositing system to a laser cutter's pen. The depositing system was created using a syringe and cannula along with 3d printed parts to connect to aircompressor.

The tricky part is that the movement of the laser cutter has to be continuous. Consequently the first trial file was a grid drawn from a continuous line, to avoid random machine movements.
