WATER HARVESTING PROJECT
Third attempt


Self-Sufficient Buildings
Graduate work
Institute for Advanced Architecture of Catalunya
MARCH 2011 - 2012

Group work
:::Ahmad Bathaei
:::Hunia Tomoum
:::Oscar Gomez

The idea was to harvest and filter Gray water, by creating a structure that is responsive to humidity and temperature. The structure, has solar panels, to generate electricity, and have the metal structure which induces an electrostatic charge which traps water from humid air down to water storage, to be used for agriculture.
