Helopolis Library


HELIOPOLIS LIBRARY
Heliopolis, Cairo

Design Studio 6
Undergraduate work
Arab Academy for Science and Technology
BSCArchE 2003 - 2008

Individual work



The challenge was to vitalize the Heliopolis Library, located on a main street "Salah Salem" in Cairo. The idea was to Replace the existing building (of no architectural value) by a building that stands out in this urban context.


Inspired by sand dunes formation, The idea was to design a building with minimum surface area exposed to sunlight, creating selfshading form, which from the outiside allow air flow to reach maximum surface area of a building as a cooling effect. Shaded areas from ecotect analysis were then traced to be all covered with glass allowing only indirect light into the building.
